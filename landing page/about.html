What inspired this project.

The idea for Gojo-Afalagi was inspired by the need for a more user-friendly and efficient platform for renting houses. As a software developer, I saw firsthand the challenges that can arise when trying to rent a house through traditional means. From finding locally to negotiating with brokers, the process can be frustrating and time-consuming. While considering these problems, I had the chance to work on Alx's portfolio project, so I decided to take matters into my own hands and create a solution that would make it easier for people to find and rent the perfect home. I used my skills in web development and Python to create Gojo-Afalagi, a platform that streamlines the rental process and provides users with a convenient and hassle-free experience.
