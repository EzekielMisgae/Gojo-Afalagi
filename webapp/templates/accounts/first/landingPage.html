What is GoJo Afalagi?

GoJo_Afalagi is a simple web application built with front end (HTML, CSS, Js) and in backend (Python / Django) that allows users/renters to see and search houses and for house owners to post available homes to rent. Users can interact easily with house owners without the interrogation of brokers.

What can you get from GoJo Afalagi?

* Registering or {% url 'webapp:login' %}g in

* Find lists of available homes for your choice

* Post your homes if you are a house owner

* Edit your posts if needed
