What is GoJo Afalagi?


GoJo-Afalagi is a web application that provides users with a platform to easily search for and view available rental properties. It allows renters to interact directly with property owners , eliminating the need for intermediaries like brokers. With GoJo-Afalagi, renters can quickly and easily browse through a variety of listings to find a home that meets their needs.



What you can do and don't?


Here are the lists you can do if you register.

  • Create an account or log in to access the site.
  • See available for rent.
  • Advertise your own homes for rent.
  • Modify your rental listings.
Learn More

Here are the lists you can't do without registering.
  • access certain features or content without creating an account or logging in
  • advertise homes for rent if you are not the owner
  • access all available rental listings without registering
  • post homes using for-tenants account.
